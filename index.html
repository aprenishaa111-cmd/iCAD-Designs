About iCAD DESIGNS

iCAD DESIGNS provides global support services for the electronics industry. Guided by experts with 15+ years of experience and IPC-CID Certification (USA), our design team delivers premium quality, cost-effective hardware blueprints. We specialize in High-Speed PCB Design, Signal Integrity, and Embedded Services across key market sectors, including Semiconductor, Aerospace, Automotive, Medical, and Military industries. By collaborating closely with NPI engineering teams, we guarantee exceptional layout yields, fast turnarounds, and robust client relationships built on pure engineering perfection.
