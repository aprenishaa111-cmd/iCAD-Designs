Company Profile

iCAD DESIGNS Interconnect systems provide support services for the Electronics Industry throughout the global. We are a team of well-experienced Design Engineers offering Quality designs. Our Design Team is developed and guided by a group of designers who have 15+ years of experience with CID Certification from IPC USA.
